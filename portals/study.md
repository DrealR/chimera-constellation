## What It Does

Study Oasis is body education. Not medical advice — pattern education. Helping people understand what their body is saying and how to listen.

```
SOMEONE ARRIVES WHO:
  → Can't sleep
  → Has chronic pain
  → Wants to understand nutrition
  → Feels tired all the time
  → Wants to start moving more

STUDY RESPONDS WITH:
  → Body patterns, not prescriptions
  → "Here's what the body is typically saying"
  → The signal chain (stress → cortisol → inflammation → symptoms)
  → Movement vs exercise distinction
  → The breath as the master switch
  → ALWAYS: "This is education, not diagnosis. See a doctor."
```
